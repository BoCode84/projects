To get your exercise log, in your browser type the following address
https://bocode-exercise-tracker.glitch.me/api/exercise/log?{userid}

Additionally, you can also expend your search by typing the date range, which is optional


To get user's exercise log, type following:
https://bocode-exercise-tracker.glitch.me/api/exercise/log?{userId}[&from][&to][&limit]

Required documents:

userId

Optional documents:

Date range (from, to)

(Date should be in format yyyy-mm-dd)

Limit

(Number)
